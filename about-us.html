ABOUT US

Disaster Alert is a non-governmental organization that works with government agencies, non-government organizations, educational institutions and business enterprises to stregthen the capacity of vulnerable communities to prepare for, respond to and recover from the impacts of natural and human-induced disasters

Our mission

To strengthen the capacity of vulnerable communities to prepare for , respond to and recover from natural and human- induced disasters.

our vision

Disaster resilient communities in Nigeria.
